ADVENTURER'S HANDBOOK

Introduction: The World of Pitchutcha

Welcome, Adventurer! This handbook is your guide to the core mechanics, advanced systems, and hidden depths of Pitchutcha (Alpha v2.0.0). This is a challenging, hub-based RPG where you will explore dangerous, procedurally-generated dungeons, fight fearsome monsters, and collect powerful loot to forge your legend.

The Core Loop: Delve, Defeat, Develop

1. The Hub & The Worlds

The Hub is your sanctuary. Between runs, you will return here to manage your character and access various vendors. To begin an adventure, select a World and a Difficulty. New Worlds are unlocked by defeating the final boss of the previous World on 'Dark' difficulty, while higher Difficulties are unlocked by completing the one before it.

2. Dungeon Exploration

Each dungeon is randomly generated and shrouded in a Fog of War. You must explore to reveal the map, where you may find:

  • Enemies: Creatures that will engage you in combat.
  • Chests: Contains gold and a chance for a random item.
  • Fountains: Restores your Health and Energy to full.
  • Traps: Hidden dangers that will damage you if stepped on.
  • Altars: Offer a choice between powerful boons and risky curses for your current run.
  • Portals: Appear after defeating all enemies on a floor, allowing you to proceed to the next level or claim victory.

3. Turn-Based Combat

Combat is tactical and turn-based. You must manage your Health and Energy (EN) to survive. Use basic attacks, guard to reduce incoming damage, or expend EN to unleash powerful skills.

Character Progression: The Path to Power

Attributes Explained

  • HP (Health Points): Your life force. Reaching zero ends your run.
  • EN (Energy): The resource used for most skills.
  • Attack (ATK): Increases the damage of your attacks and skills.
  • Defense (DEF): Reduces incoming damage. The more you have, the less effective each point becomes.
  • Luck: Increases your chance to find higher quality loot and your chance to land a critical hit.
  • Critical Damage: The damage multiplier applied on a critical hit. Starts at a base of +50%.

The Skill Tree & Mastery

Each time you level up, you gain a Skill Point. Spend these in the Skill Book to unlock powerful active abilities and permanent passive stat boosts for your chosen class. Furthermore, as you use your active skills in combat, they will gain Mastery XP, eventually leveling up to become even more potent.

The Armory: Understanding Your Loot

Loot is the heart of your power. Understanding its nuances is key to success.

Item Qualities & Affixes

  • Perks: Percentage-based stat bonuses that can appear on gear. A high-level Perk can be more valuable than flat stats.
  • Set Items: Equipping multiple items from the same named set (e.g., Guardian's Resolve) will activate powerful cumulative bonuses.
  • Primal Items: Legendary or higher items have a small chance to be Primal, indicated by a glowing white border. Primal items always roll with maximum-level Perks.

Endgame & Advanced Systems

Ascension: The Prestige Cycle

Upon reaching the level cap (starting at 50), you can Ascend. This resets your character's level, skills, and gold, but grants you Prestige and Ascension Points. These points are spent in the Ascension Grid to unlock permanent, account-wide bonuses, making each subsequent journey to the level cap faster and more powerful.

The Gauntlet & Challenge Rifts

Once you've mastered normal dungeons, test your might in these endgame modes. The Gauntlet is an endless dungeon where you climb as high as possible for rank. Challenge Rifts are weekly trials with fixed gear where you compete for the fastest completion time.

Dynamic Run Modifiers

  • Run Streaks: Completing the same dungeon map consecutively triggers special run types (Echoing, Resonant, Chaotic) with increased rewards and dangers.
  • Synergy Breaks: Slaying elite foes fills your Synergy Meter. At 100%, you get to choose one of three powerful, temporary boons for your run.
  • The Nemesis System: If a normal or elite enemy defeats you, they may be promoted into a named Nemesis. This powerful foe will remember you and may ambush you in a future run.
