THE GENESIS UPDATE

Note: This changelog serves as a comprehensive overview of all systems implemented in the current Alpha build. Welcome to the world of Pitchutcha!

SYSTEMS OVERHAUL & FEATURE INTEGRATION

The World & Core Gameplay Loop

  • World Progression: The adventure is no longer confined to a single forest. Unlock new Worlds with unique visual themes, enemies, and powerful bosses by conquering the previous world's 'Dark' difficulty.
  • Procedural Dungeons: Every run is unique! Dungeons are procedurally generated, complete with random layouts, enemy placements, and hidden treasures.
  • Fog of War: Each floor is shrouded in darkness. Explore to reveal the map, but beware what lurks in the shadows.
  • Interactive Environments: Discover and interact with treasure chests, healing fountains, dangerous traps, cursed chests, and mysterious altars that offer a choice between great power and dire consequences.

Character Progression & Combat

  • Dual-Class System: Choose your path as a resilient Warrior or an arcane Mage. Your class can be switched at any time from the Inventory screen.
  • Deep Skill Trees: Each class now has a unique skill tree. Invest skill points earned from leveling up to unlock powerful active abilities and game-changing passive bonuses.
  • Skill Mastery: Using skills in combat grants mastery XP, increasing their level and unlocking bonus effects over time.
  • Tactical Turn-Based Combat: A complete combat system has been implemented, featuring energy management, skill cooldowns, and a wide array of status effects (buffs and debuffs).

The Armory: An Advanced Loot System

  • Tiered Rarity System: A comprehensive 9-tier rarity system has been implemented: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic, Artifact, Divine, and the rule-breaking Exotic rarity.
  • Unique Items: The most powerful and rarest items in the game have been added. These items possess unparalleled power and are visually distinct.
  • Item Affixes: Gear can now roll with powerful Perks (percentage-based stat boosts) and is assigned a Gear Score to help evaluate its power.
  • Set Items: Collect and equip pieces from the same item set to unlock powerful cumulative bonuses.
  • Primal Gear: High-rarity items have a small chance to drop as Primal, featuring perfectly rolled level 5 Perks.

The Hub: Your Base of Operations

  • The Forge: Upgrade your equipment's base stats using Arcane Dust. Reforge and Infusion systems are planned for a future update.
  • Marketplace & Gambler: A full-featured shop with a buyback system is now open. Feeling lucky? Visit the Gambler to trade gold for a chance at rare items.
  • Transmutation Cube: Combine items to create new, more powerful ones. Discover hidden recipes to craft unique gear.
  • Appearance & Wardrobe: Customize your character's look with cosmetic skins purchased from the Diamond Shop.

Endgame & Replayability Systems

  • Ascension & Prestige: Reach the level cap to Ascend, resetting your level to gain permanent, account-wide bonuses through the Ascension Grid. This is the core long-term progression system.
  • The Gauntlet: Test your endurance in an endless dungeon mode where difficulty scales with each floor. Climb as high as you can to secure your spot on the leaderboard.
  • Challenge Rifts: Compete in weekly challenges with a fixed character loadout on a static dungeon layout. Race against the clock for the best time.
  • Dynamic Run Modifiers:
    • Run Streaks: Completing the same dungeon consecutively triggers special run types: Echoing, Resonant, and Chaotic.
    • Anomalies: Chaotic runs are afflicted with powerful world-altering effects.
    • Synergy Breaks & Boons: Slaying elite enemies fills your Synergy Meter. When full, you are granted a choice of a powerful, run-altering blessing. Using skills frequently will attune you to them, allowing you to empower future item drops with a Boon.
    • The Nemesis System: Be warned! Defeat at the hands of a common foe may see them return in a future run as a powerful, named Nemesis seeking to hunt you down.

Community & Competition

  • Guild System: Create or join a guild, recruit NPC members, and work together to complete challenging weekly Guild Bounties for massive rewards.
  • The Arena: Engage in asynchronous PvP combat. Climb the ranks, earn Arena Marks for the exclusive shop, and compete for top spots in the seasonal leaderboard.
  • Comprehensive Leaderboards: Track your performance against other players across numerous categories, including Gauntlet progression, combat prowess, and overall prestige.
  • Quest Board & Login Rewards: Complete daily and weekly quests for valuable resources. Log in each day to claim progressively better rewards.
