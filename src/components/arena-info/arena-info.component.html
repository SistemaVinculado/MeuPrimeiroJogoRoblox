ARENA INFORMATION

How to Play

Welcome to the Arena, a place where champions are forged. Here's how it works:

  • Arena Tickets: Each battle costs one Arena Ticket. You regenerate tickets over time.
  • Challengers: You will be presented with three opponents near your current rank. Defeating them will increase your rank, while losing will decrease it.
  • Ranking Up: The higher your opponent's rank, the more you stand to gain upon victory, and the less you'll lose in defeat.
  • Arena Marks: Win or lose, every battle earns you Arena Marks, which can be spent in the exclusive Arena Shop. Winning streaks grant bonus Marks!
  • Revenge: If you are defeated, you can challenge that opponent to a rematch from the Battle Logs tab without spending a ticket. You only get one chance!

End of Season Rewards

At the end of each season, you will receive rewards based on your final Arena Rank. Strive for the top to earn the most prestigious prizes!
